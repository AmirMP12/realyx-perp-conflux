# User Guide

Welcome to Realyx on **Conflux eSpace**. This guide covers everything you need to start trading RWAs with leverage.

## 1. Getting Started

### Network Setup
1. **Add Conflux eSpace Testnet** to your wallet (MetaMask, OKX, etc.):
   - **Network Name**: Conflux eSpace (Testnet)
   - **RPC URL**: `https://evmtestnet.confluxrpc.com`
   - **Chain ID**: `71`
   - **Currency Symbol**: `CFX`
   - **Block Explorer**: `https://evmtestnet.confluxscan.net`

2. **Get Testnet CFX**: Visit the [Conflux Faucet](https://evmfaucet.confluxnetwork.org/) to receive gas for your transactions.

## 2. Trading

### Opening a Position
1. Navigate to the **Trade** tab.
2. Select your desired market (e.g., BTC/USD, Gold/USD).
3. Choose **Long** (price goes up) or **Short** (price goes down).
4. Enter your **Order Size** (in USDC) and **Leverage** (up to 10x).
5. Click **Open Position** and confirm the transaction in your wallet.

### Managing Positions
- **Adjust Margin**: You can add collateral to increase your Health Factor or withdraw excess margin.
- **Close Position**: Use the **Close** button in the positions table to exit your trade at the current market price.
- **Stop Loss / Take Profit**: Set trigger prices to automatically close your position and lock in profits or minimize losses.

## 3. Liquidity Providing (Staking)

### Staking USDC
1. Go to the **Vault** page.
2. Enter the amount of USDC you wish to deposit.
3. Click **Deposit**. You will receive Vault Shares representing your portion of the pool.
4. LPs earn fees from traders and funding rates.

### Unstaking
1. Request a withdrawal on the Vault page.
2. Wait for the **Cooldown Period** (typically 7 days).
3. After the cooldown, click **Execute Withdrawal** to receive your USDC and earned rewards.
