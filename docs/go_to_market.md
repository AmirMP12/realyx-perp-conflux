# Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy

Realyx aims to become the premier Perpetual DEX for RWAs on **Conflux eSpace**. This document outlines our strategy for launch and ecosystem growth.

## 1. Phase 1: Community Bootstrapping (Testnet)
- **Faucet Incentive Program**: Reward early testers who provide feedback on the trading UX using testnet CFX and USDC.
- **Trading Competition**: Host a weekly leaderboard competition with OG roles and future whitelist rewards for top PnL traders.
- **Conflux Ecosystem Synergy**: Partner with existing Conflux projects (faucets, wallets, bridges) to drive cross-protocol awareness.

## 2. Phase 2: Liquidity Genesis (Mainnet Launch)
- **Early LP Incentives**: Launch the `VaultCore` with boosted rewards (yield + token incentives) for the first $1M of USDC liquidity.
- **RWA Focus**: Differentiate from general DEXs by highlighting unique markets like **Gold**, **Silver**, and **Global Equities** futures.
- **Security Audit Promotion**: Publicly release a comprehensive audit report (e.g., from PeckShield or similar) to build institutional trust.

## 3. Phase 3: Institutional & API Integration
- **Liquidity Provider Onboarding**: Partner with market makers to ensure deep liquidity and tight spreads across all RWA pairs.
- **Developer Grants**: Provide documentation and funding for developers to build automated trading bots and vaults on top of the Realyx API.
- **White-Label Solutions**: Enable other protocols to integrate Realyx's perps natively into their dashboards.

## 4. Marketing Channels
- **X (Twitter)**: Real-time protocol updates and market analysis.
- **Discord**: High-engagement community home for traders and LPs.
- **Medium/Substack**: Deep-dives into RWA tokenization and DeFi architecture.

## 5. Key Success Metrics (KPIs)
| Metric | Goal (6 Months) |
|--------|-----------------|
| Total Value Locked (TVL) | $5M+ |
| 24h Trading Volume | $50M+ |
| Active Traders (Daily) | 500+ |
| Community Size | 10k+ Members |

*Execution of this strategy will be dynamic and community-led through future governance.*
